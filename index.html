Postero Privacy Policy

Effective 14 January 2024

Postero (hereinafter referred to as the "App")

This Privacy Policy is designed to help you understand how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our desktop app and browser extension (collectively referred to as the "Service").

Information We Do Not Collect

We are committed to protecting your privacy, and we want to make it clear that we do not collect any personal data from users of our App. This includes, but is not limited to:

  • Personal Information: We do not collect any personally identifiable information, such as names, email addresses, or phone numbers.
  • Browsing History: We do not track or store your browsing history.
  • Location Information: We do not collect information about your physical location.
  • Payment Details: We do not process any payments, and we do not collect payment information.

Information Collected Automatically

While using our Service, we may collect certain information automatically, but this information is not personally identifiable. This may include:

  • Usage Information: We may collect information about your interactions with the App, such as the features you use and the frequency of usage.
  • Technical Information: We may collect technical information such as your device type, operating system, and browser type.

How We Use Information

The non-personal information we collect is used for the sole purpose of improving and enhancing the functionality of our App. We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer this information to third parties.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the information we collect from unauthorized access or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

Updates to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time, and any changes will be effective immediately upon posting the updated version on our website. You are encouraged to periodically review this page for the latest information on our privacy practices.
